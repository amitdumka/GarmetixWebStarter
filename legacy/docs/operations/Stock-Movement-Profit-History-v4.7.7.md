# Stock Movement Profit History v4.7.7

After deployment:

1. Open **Stock Reports**.
2. Search a product or barcode.
3. Click **History**.
4. Verify rows for purchase inward, sales, sale returns and purchase returns.
5. Confirm current stock value equals current quantity multiplied by weighted average cost.
6. Confirm sale profit/loss equals sale amount less cost of goods sold.
7. Export movement CSV and compare with invoice activity.

## Notes

Profit/loss is calculated from sale movement rows using the stock movement cost price at the sale date. Purchase returns and stock operations affect stock quantity/value but do not create sale profit. Sale returns reverse the sales margin impact.

# HR Employee Master Operation Guide v4.9.21

Open `/hr` and maintain employee master data before attendance or payroll.

## Required for production

- Employee code, or leave blank to auto-generate.
- Name, mobile, Aadhaar and joining date.
- Store assignment through workspace.
- Department and designation.
- Salary type and amount.
- Bank account and IFSC for salary payout.
- Emergency contact and photo for ID-card readiness.

## Employee lifecycle

Use the employee status field:

- `Active` for working employees.
- `On Leave` for temporary leave.
- `Resigned`, `Terminated` or `Inactive` to stop active payroll/attendance processing.

When an exit status is selected, fill leaving date and exit reason.

## ID Card

From the Employees table, click **ID Card** to preview and print the card. The card uses company, store, employee code, photo, designation, emergency contact and blood group.

# Stage 8H Package 4 - Tailoring and Alteration Pro (v4.7.3)

This package rebuilds the tailoring workspace into a more production-ready garment-service workflow.

## Tailoring / Stitching

- Customer mobile lookup before order entry.
- Multiple stitching items like sale-invoice line entry.
- Service order with expected delivery date, measurements, instructions, vendor and vendor cost.
- Status flow: Ordered, Sent to Vendor, Processing, Ready, Delivered, Invoiced and Completed.
- Service invoice conversion with 5% GST, line discount, customer receipts and two-copy printing.

## Alteration

- Separate alteration workflow.
- Customer and original sale invoice are selected first.
- Altered invoice item/product is linked to the alteration order.
- Customer charge can be higher than vendor cost.
- In-house alteration expense can be absorbed by store and applied once to the linked source stock item cost for margin impact.

## Delivery control

- Delivery board separates today, tomorrow, late, ready and completed orders.
- Quick status buttons allow marking Processing, Ready and Delivered.

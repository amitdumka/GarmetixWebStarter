# Purchase and Vendor Payment Workflow v4.6.7

## New inward

Use **Purchase → New Inward** or `/purchase/new` to enter supplier invoice, products, stock receiving and first payment as a full page.

## Vendor payment

Use **Purchase → Vendor Payments** or `/vendor-payments` to:

- pay against an outstanding purchase invoice,
- record a vendor advance payment,
- review recent vendor payments and linked vouchers.

# Store Day Opening / Closing v4.9.8

## Open store day

1. Select correct workspace store from top bar.
2. Open **Store Day Open / Close**.
3. Select date.
4. Enter opening balance and/or opening cash notes.
5. Click **Open / Update Day**.

## Close store day

1. Open **Store Day Open / Close**.
2. Review book cash.
3. Enter physical cash notes.
4. Click **Close Day + Save Petty Cash**.
5. Print petty cash sheet.

## Store holiday / closed day

Use **Mark Holiday / Closed**.

This carries yesterday's closing to both opening and closing, creates petty cash sheet, and blocks entries for that date.

## Mandatory rule

Store Manager and Billing/Sales users must open the day before entries. After closing, entries are blocked.

Admin and Accountant users are not blocked by this guard.

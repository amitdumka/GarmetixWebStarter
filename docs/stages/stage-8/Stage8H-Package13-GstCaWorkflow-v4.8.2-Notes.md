# Stage 8H Package 13 - GST CA Workflow Polish (v4.8.2)

This package builds on Brevo SMTP and the GST review/share automation.

## Included

- GST Returns and GST Reports now share one reusable Accountant/CA contact.
- Accountant/CA email, name, WhatsApp number and note can be saved from the GST send dialog.
- Saved contact auto-fills both GST Returns and GST Reports.
- Recent GST share history is visible in the GST Returns/Reports sharing panels.
- GST send flow still requires review/confirmation before email is sent.
- WhatsApp share link remains available after successful email delivery.

## Verification

1. Open GST Reports.
2. Click **Review & Send**.
3. Enter CA email/name/WhatsApp number.
4. Click **Save as default CA contact**.
5. Close and open GST Returns.
6. Click **Send to CA** and verify the same contact auto-fills.
7. Send test report/return and confirm recent share log appears.
